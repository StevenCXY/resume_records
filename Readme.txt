📖 How to Use
Add a Target: Go to the "🚀 Tracker" tab. Enter your Goal (e.g., Data Analyst) and Remark (e.g., LinkedIn), then click "Add to list".

Log an Application: * Click the "✓" button on a task to open the floating Mini-Widget.

Drag the widget anywhere on your screen.

Click "+1" every time you submit a resume.

Click "✕" on the widget to return to the main dashboard.

View Progress: * Check the "📊 Dashboard" tab for your overall progress.

Click the "📊" icon next to a specific task to view its individual performance.
